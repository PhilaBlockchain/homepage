Problem Statement

Pila has identifed a series of problems with the current healthcare system. These issues effect all members of the system: the Department of Public Health, Patients and healthcare providers.

Problem with the current system

  • Patients' health information is stored in silos across multiple healthcare providers
  • Limited or lack of data integration creates gaps in patient health records and histories.
  • Patients repeatedly fill out the same basic health information for each new provider.
  • Patients have little to no access to real time personal health records and indicators
  • High rates of treatment default by patients on chronic medications with little to no adherence strategies
  • Ineffective incentives to encourage healthier lifestyle choices, preventative healthcare and behavior change
  • Ineffective electronic healthcare systems for tracking and measuring effects of public health campaigns
